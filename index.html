A LITTLE ABOUT EMERALD CHINA

Emerald China provides the freshest ingredients and homemade-chinese preparations. In business since 1988, Emerald China has now become a favorite among Duluth's community because of its warm and friendly atmosphere. The restaurant has even garnered praise among all food connoisseurs for it's raised standards of chinese cuisine.

Emerald China's dedication to health and a love for the local community that Emerald China continues their mission to make fresh, high-quality, affordable food for everyone to enjoy.

Thank you for making Emerald China a part of your day.
